# Decentralized Food System Resilience and Security Platform

A comprehensive blockchain-based platform designed to enhance food system resilience, reduce waste, and improve food security through decentralized coordination and incentive mechanisms.

## Overview

This platform consists of five interconnected smart contracts that work together to create a robust, community-driven food system:

### 1. Local Food Production Support Contract (`local-production.clar`)
- **Purpose**: Incentivizes and supports local farming and food processing capacity
- **Features**:
    - Producer registration and verification
    - Production capacity tracking
    - Yield reporting and rewards
    - Resource sharing coordination
    - Seasonal planning support

### 2. Food Waste Reduction Coordination Contract (`waste-reduction.clar`)
- **Purpose**: Connects surplus food sources with food banks and hungry populations
- **Features**:
    - Surplus food listing and matching
    - Donation tracking and verification
    - Pickup and delivery coordination
    - Impact measurement and reporting
    - Donor incentive programs

### 3. Nutrition Education and Cooking Contract (`nutrition-education.clar`)
- **Purpose**: Promotes healthy eating and food preparation skills through education
- **Features**:
    - Educational content management
    - Skill certification tracking
    - Community cooking events
    - Recipe sharing and rating
    - Nutritionist verification system

### 4. Urban Agriculture Development Contract (`urban-agriculture.clar`)
- **Purpose**: Supports community gardens and urban farming initiatives
- **Features**:
    - Garden plot allocation and management
    - Resource pooling and sharing
    - Harvest tracking and distribution
    - Community event coordination
    - Equipment lending programs

### 5. Food Safety Inspection Contract (`food-safety.clar`)
- **Purpose**: Ensures food production and distribution meets safety standards
- **Features**:
    - Inspector certification and management
    - Inspection scheduling and reporting
    - Compliance tracking and scoring
    - Violation reporting and remediation
    - Safety standard updates

- Stakeholder representation across all contracts

- Achievement badges and certifications

## Technical Architecture

### Smart Contract Design
- **Language**: Clarity (Stacks blockchain)
- **Architecture**: Modular, independent contracts
- **Data Storage**: On-chain for critical data, off-chain for large content
- **Security**: Built-in safety checks and validation

### Key Data Structures
- Producer/participant profiles
- Resource inventories
- Transaction histories
- Reputation scores
- Geographic mapping data

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- Clarinet CLI installed
- Node.js and npm
- Basic understanding of Clarity smart contracts

### Installation
\`\`\`bash
git clone <repository-url>
cd food-system-platform
npm install
clarinet check
\`\`\`

### Testing
\`\`\`bash
npm test
\`\`\`

### Deployment
\`\`\`bash
clarinet deploy
\`\`\`

## Usage Examples

### For Food Producers
1. Register as a producer in the local production contract
2. Report yields and available surplus
3. Coordinate with waste reduction for surplus distribution
4. Participate in safety inspections

### For Community Members
1. Join urban agriculture initiatives
2. Access nutrition education resources
3. Participate in food rescue operations
4. Contribute to community gardens

### For Organizations
1. Register as food banks or distribution centers
2. Schedule safety inspections
3. Coordinate large-scale food rescue operations
4. Provide educational resources

## Impact Metrics

The platform tracks several key performance indicators:
- **Food Waste Reduction**: Tons of food rescued and redistributed
- **Local Production**: Increase in local food production capacity
- **Education Reach**: Number of people educated on nutrition and cooking
- **Urban Agriculture**: Square footage of urban growing space activated
- **Safety Compliance**: Percentage of food operations meeting safety standards
